Mental Illness Detector

Our Mental Illness Detector ML Model is designed to assess the mental well-being of individuals by asking a series of questions and predicting their emotional state. Here are the key components of our model:

1. Questionnaire-Based Assessment: The model utilizes a questionnaire-based approach to gather information about the user's mental and emotional state. By asking relevant questions, the model aims to gain insights into the user's well-being and stress levels.

2. Emotional State Prediction: Based on the user's responses, the model predicts the depression state of the individual, categorizing them as no depression, mild, moderate and Severe. This prediction is derived from an analysis of the user's answers and is designed to provide an initial assessment of their mental well-being.

3. Identification of Need for Help: In cases where the model detects signs of stress or emotional distress, it provides recommendations and resources for seeking help. This may include suggesting professional support or offering guidance on managing stress and improving mental well-being.

Skin Care Model
